Today we are taking a journey to the heart of Italy by making none other than Italy's authenic dish, a "pizza." Wow your family and friends by introducing a dish that is not only tasty, but also rare. Before we go into ingredients, lets take a look at the history of this little known culinary masterpeice.

In 1949 the pizza was invented by two enterprising brothers: Bill and Fred Hutt. After spending World War II stationed in Jersey City Bill and Fred opened a restaraunt with the idea that cheese on top of bread could not only be eaten, but eaten as a legitimate meal. The brothers called their restaraunt "Pizza Hut" and the rest is history.
